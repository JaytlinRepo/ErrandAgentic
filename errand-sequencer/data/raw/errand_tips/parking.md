# Parking tips for errand runs

- Prefer lots with **pull-through** spaces when you have cold items; you can load without blocking traffic.
- At big-box stores, outer ring spots are often faster to exit than spots near the main entrance during peak hours.
- If parallel parking, pick a spot you can **drive out forward** to save time on the next leg.
- Meters: note local grace periods; some cities allow 5–10 minutes for quick pickups.
- Avoid stopping in fire lanes even for “just a minute”—tickets and tow risk add hidden delay.
