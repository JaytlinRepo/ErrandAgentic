# Returns, exchanges, and pickups

- Bring **receipts or order numbers** ready on your phone before entering the line—fumbling at the counter adds real delay.
- Big-box return desks cluster traffic near the front; off-peak weekday slots reduce wait more than choosing a “faster” parking row.
- If you have both a return and a purchase, check whether the store allows **return + new purchase in one visit** to avoid double queuing.
- Curbside pickup: confirm the app shows “ready” before detouring; arriving early often means waiting anyway.
