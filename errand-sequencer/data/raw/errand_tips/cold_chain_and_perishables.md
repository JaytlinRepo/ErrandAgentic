# Cold chain and perishable errands

- Do **frozen and refrigerated items last** when possible so they spend less time in a hot car.
- If you must buy cold items early, bring an **insulated bag** and keep the car cool; sequence shortest hops after the grocery leg.
- Ice cream and delicate produce suffer most from multi-stop loops—avoid sitting in traffic between freezer and home.
- For hot food pickup, plan it **immediately before** the drive home so quality holds.
