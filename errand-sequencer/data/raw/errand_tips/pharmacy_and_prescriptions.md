# Pharmacy and prescriptions

- **Refill requests** may take time to prepare—call ahead or use the app before you drive to avoid a second trip.
- Drive-through pharmacy lanes can be faster than inside when you only need pickup; inside may be faster if you have questions for the pharmacist.
- If insurance requires a prior authorization, same-day pickup is often unrealistic; plan pickup after notification.
- For controlled substances, ID and timing rules are strict—bring ID and go during staffed hours.
