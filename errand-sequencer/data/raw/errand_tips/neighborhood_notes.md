# Neighborhood notes (generic patterns)

- **Strip malls**: Cluster errands in one plaza before crossing arterials; left turns across heavy traffic cost more time than distance suggests.
- **Urban cores**: Prefer **perimeter parking** + short walk if garage ingress queues are common on weekends.
- **Suburban collectors**: Routes that “loop” the same retail corridor twice usually waste 5–15 minutes; reorder to visit all stores on one side before crossing.
- **Weather**: Light rain often shifts crowds into covered big-box entrances—lines may form at single entry doors.
