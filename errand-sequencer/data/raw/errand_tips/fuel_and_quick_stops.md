# Fuel and quick vehicle stops

- If fuel is low, **fill up when you are already near a station** on the route rather than making a separate trip later.
- Pay-at-pump is usually faster than going inside; use that when you are time-boxed.
- Car washes and vacuum bays often have queues on weekends—weekday mid-morning is typically lighter.
- EV charging: prefer stops that overlap with an errand (grocery, mall) so charging time is not “dead” time.
