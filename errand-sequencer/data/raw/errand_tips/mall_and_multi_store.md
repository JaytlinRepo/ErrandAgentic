# Malls and multi-store visits

- **Park once** and complete all interior stops before moving the car to another lot—re-parking can cost 10–20 minutes in busy centers.
- Anchor stores often have different entrances; map which entrance is closest to your first and last interior stop.
- Food courts peak at **noon–1 PM** on weekdays and **5–8 PM** on weekends—lines for quick service can spill into your next leg.
- If carrying bags from multiple stores, a **central bench or locker** (where available) can reduce backtracking to the car.
