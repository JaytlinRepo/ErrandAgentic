## Georgia Alcohol Sales
Georgia has well-known Sunday and nighttime constraints for alcohol retail sales. Exact rules vary by county and municipality and should always be verified with live data when relevant. Do not assert specific alcohol sale hours without confirming current local ordinances. This is a high-value reminder to surface when alcohol purchase is part of an errand list.

## Pharmacy Hours Vary Widely
Pharmacy hours differ significantly by location. 24-hour pharmacies exist but are not universal. Standalone pharmacies often close earlier than pharmacies inside grocery stores or big box stores. Always confirm pharmacy hours with live Maps data for the specific location. Never assume a pharmacy is open based on the parent store hours.

## Returns Policy Patterns
Returns policies are merchant-specific and change periodically. Static documents should note patterns not guarantees. Most big box retailers require receipt or order confirmation for returns. Electronics returns windows are typically shorter than general merchandise. High-value items may require manager approval and longer processing time.

## Government Office Appointments
DMV, county tag offices, probate courts, and social services offices often require appointments and have strict hours. Sequencing should always treat government offices as hard anchors. Walk-in availability varies by location and day. Always verify appointment requirements before building a route that depends on a government office visit.

## Cash vs Card and ID Requirements
Some errands require specific payment methods or identification. Notary services often require payment at time of service. Certain pharmacy pickups for controlled substances require ID. Storage unit access may require the account holder to be present. Note these requirements as part of errand planning to avoid wasted trips.

## Costco and Sam's Club Membership
Warehouse clubs require active membership for entry. Costco accepts Visa credit cards only at checkout. Sam's Club accepts most payment methods. Verify membership is current before routing to a warehouse club. A lapsed membership discovered at the door wastes significant time.

## Apple Store and Best Buy Appointments
Apple Store technical support and Genius Bar visits require appointments. Walk-in availability is unpredictable. Best Buy Geek Squad has similar appointment dynamics. Sequence these stops only when appointment is confirmed. Treat confirmed tech support appointments as hard time anchors.

## Post Office and Shipping Services
Post office hours vary by location. Main branches have longer hours than smaller locations. UPS Store and FedEx locations inside Staples or other retailers may have earlier closing times than standalone locations. Always verify hours for the specific shipping location. Package pickup requires bringing the notice slip and valid ID.

## Prescription Pickup Timing
New prescriptions and controlled substance refills may have processing time requirements. Same-day fills are not always guaranteed. Calling ahead to confirm a prescription is ready before routing to the pharmacy saves significant time. Some medications require pharmacist consultation which adds time to the pickup.

## Survey and Specialty Services
Services like car registration, passport applications, and professional license renewals have specific document and timing requirements. These are high-stakes errands where arriving without the correct documents means a wasted trip. Always verify requirements before sequencing these stops. Treat them as high-priority anchors with documentation checklists.
