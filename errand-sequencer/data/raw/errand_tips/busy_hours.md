# Busy hours and timing

- **Grocery stores**: Often busiest **weekday 4–7 PM** and **Saturday mid-morning**. Sunday evenings can be lighter in some suburbs.
- **Big-box retail**: Peaks on **Saturday afternoons**; weekday mornings are usually calmer.
- **Post office / UPS / FedEx**: Lunch rush **11 AM–1 PM** and first/last hour of the day see longer lines.
- **Pharmacies**: Monday mornings after weekend refill backlogs can be slower; mid-afternoon weekdays often shorter waits.
- **School zones**: If routing near schools, avoid **7:30–8:30 AM** and **2:30–4 PM** on weekdays for predictable drive times.
