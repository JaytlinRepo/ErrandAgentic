## Warehouse Club Trips
Costco and Sam's Club trips need 45 to 75 minutes all-in including parking, shopping, and cart unload. Never treat warehouse clubs as quick stops. Schedule them early in the day before crowds build. Pair with nearby stops on the same corridor to justify the time investment.
---
- **Grocery stores**: Often busiest **weekday 4–7 PM** and **Saturday mid-morning**. Sunday evenings can be lighter in some suburbs.
- **Big-box retail**: Peaks on **Saturday afternoons**; weekday mornings are usually calmer.
- **Post office / UPS / FedEx**: Lunch rush **11 AM–1 PM** and first/last hour of the day see longer lines.

## Warehouse Club Internal Flow
Costco and Sam's Club have long internal paths from entrance to checkout to exit. Cart return and car loading add time. The experience is fundamentally different from a grocery store. Never estimate a warehouse club trip at under 45 minutes.

## Costco and Sam's Club Membership
Warehouse clubs require active membership for entry. Costco accepts Visa credit cards only at checkout. Sam's Club accepts most payment methods. Verify membership is current before routing to a warehouse club. A lapsed membership discovered at the door wastes significant time.

# Fuel and quick vehicle stops
